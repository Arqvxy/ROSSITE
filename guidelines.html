Server Rules

  • Channel Misusage: Misuse of channels is prohibited as there's designated channels for each task.
  • Spamming & Flooding: Excessive spamming of any sort will lead to consequences.
  • Explicit Content: Explicit content includes, but is not limited to, inappropriate messages and media.
  • Hate Speech: Using any sorts of hate speech is strictly prohibited.
  • Advertising: Advertising of any kind within our communications server is prohibited.
  • Respect: Please be respectful of all members within our community.
  • Politics and Controversy: Political and controversial topics are not strictly prohibited.
  • Staff Impersonation: Impersonating any staff members will lead to immediate removal.
  • Threats: Any threats made towards anyone will lead to permanent removal.
  • Server Evasion: Evading the server to bypass a mute or ban will lead to a permanent ban.
  • Personal Information: Disclosing personal information about someone will be punished.

Game Rules

  • Fair Play: Cheating or exploiting bugs will result in a ban.
  • Respect: Be respectful to other players during PVP battles.
  • Teamwork: Coordinate with your team to achieve victory.
  • No Harassment: Harassment of any kind will not be tolerated.
