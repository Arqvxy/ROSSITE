Welcome to Return of Sabrina

Inspired by the Netflix series "Chilling Adventures of Sabrina," this game takes you into a dark, mystical world where witches, warlocks, and other supernatural beings battle for supremacy. Choose your faction, cast spells, and engage in intense PVP battles to claim dominance in the dark realms.
