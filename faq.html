Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. What is Return of Sabrina?

Return of Sabrina is an online multiplayer game inspired by the popular Netflix series "Chilling Adventures of Sabrina," where players engage in supernatural adventures, combat, and quests.

2. How do I join the game?

To join, simply create an account and choose your faction. Follow the instructions on the homepage to get started.

3. Are there any special rules for gameplay?

Yes, there are server and game rules that you should follow to ensure a fair and enjoyable experience. Check the guidelines section for more information.

4. Can I report a player?

Yes, if you experience any issues with another player, you can report them through our support page.

5. How can I get support?

For support, visit the 'Support' section on the navigation bar, where you can contact our team directly.
